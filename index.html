Efpalinos Consulting

Efpalinos Consulting is a Cyprus transportation planning and engineering consulting company

About

Efpalinos Meletitiki was formed in 1999 to provide in Cyprus a single source for a comprehensive range of high quality services in the field of Civil Engineering and Transportation. Single sourcing is particularly important for providing consulting services within the working environment of Cyprus - a relatively limited geographical area, but applying state of the art technologies and knowledge is mostly necessary to serve the increasing demands of local economy at the highest standards.

Civil Engineering

Community service, intelligent development and quality of life. For us, this is what civil engineering is about. As we shape our communities, we enjoy both “pure” engineering as well as agency coordination and public involvement. As civil engineers, our clients task us to develop creative, cost-effective solutions to oftentimes challenging problems. We routinely provide solutions for coordination of multiple private and public utilities in densely populated activity centers, for protecting critical infrastructure from rising flood waters, for sequencing the work in order to minimize the construction schedule and for providing aesthetic and cost-effective, functional designs. Efpalinos Consulting provides comprehensive civil engineering services, including project development and management, design and construction oversight for all types of infrastructure projects. Our experience includes land and site development, public and private utility systems, drainage studies and streetscape improvement projects. Our Civil Engineering services include:

  • Land Development
  • Master Planning
  • Roadway design
  • Site Development
  • Utility System Design

Transportation Engineering

Today’s motorists demand transportation facilities that are safe, attractive, easy-to-navigate and efficient. As transportation providers across the country plan solutions that increase or optimize the capacity of transportation networks, creative design approaches are required to balance design with the environment and future growth. At Efpalinos Consulting, we understand land-use impacts of transportation improvements, the need for sustainable development and transportation’s role in improving a community’s quality of life. Our full range of transportation engineering services includes planning and design of highways, roadways and transit systems. We know our communities because we live in them and we help facilitate stakeholder support about improvements.

Our communications with engineers and end users help bring about effective solutions. We work with the latest analysis and design software for geometric design, hydraulic modeling, cost estimating, project scheduling and traffic management. Our experience allows us to manage and execute complex transportation projects through design excellence, dependable project delivery and exemplar client service.

Parking Consulting

“Smart” parking solutions consider the interaction of cars and people. Our understanding of the function and operations of parking places, together with our engineering expertise, results in parking facilities that are integrated, economical, durable and attractive. Our engineers work closely with owners and architects to develop parking facility designs that meet their operational and management needs.

A well-planned and functioning parking place is more profitable, better utilized and, of course, preferred by customers. We handle all aspects of parking facility design, from master planning to design and through construction. The internal workings of a parking place, such as parking access and revenue control equipment and vehicular and pedestrian way-finding graphics, are an integral part of the parking place design process.

Our custom designs take all of these aspects into account to provide a superior customer experience. When the facility is complete, we provide operational consulting services to help keep the parking place in working order.

Sustainable Design & Renewable Energy

Sustainable Design. Sustainable Engineering. Green Buildings. These terms mean the same thing: creating buildings that solve environmental problems, both for the physical environment, through such criteria as energy efficiency and materials selection, and for the indoor environmental quality of building occupants. We have long been committed to sustainability and to finding ways of advancing the field. Our interdisciplinary teams have created design solutions, in collaboration with architects and other engineers, that reduce fossil fuel use, use recycled materials and use locally available materials in order to help meet sustainable design goals.
